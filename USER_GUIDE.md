# Donatechain User Guide 🎁

Welcome to Donatechain - a blockchain-powered donation platform on Algorand!

## For Donors 💝

### Getting Started

#### 1. Install a Wallet

You need an Algorand wallet to donate. Choose one:

**Pera Wallet** (Recommended)
- Download: [perawallet.app](https://perawallet.app/)
- Available on iOS, Android, and Web
- Easy to use, secure

**Defly Wallet**
- Download: [defly.app](https://defly.app/)
- Available on iOS, Android, and Web
- Advanced features

#### 2. Get TestNet ALGO (For Testing)

If using TestNet:
1. Open your wallet and copy your address
2. Visit [Algorand TestNet Dispenser](https://bank.testnet.algorand.network/)
3. Paste your address
4. Click "Dispense"
5. Wait 10 seconds - you'll receive 10 TestNet ALGO

**Note**: TestNet ALGO has no real value - it's for testing only!

#### 3. Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit the Donatechain website
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
3. Choose your wallet (Pera or Defly)
4. Approve the connection in your wallet app
5. You're connected! Your address will show in the navbar

### Making a Donation

1. **Browse Campaigns**
   - View active campaigns on the home page
   - See campaign details, goals, and progress

2. **Select a Campaign**
   - Click on any campaign card
   - Review campaign information
   - Check how much has been raised

3. **Donate**
   - Click the "Donate" button
   - Review the transaction in your wallet
   - Confirm the transaction
   - Wait for confirmation (5-10 seconds)

4. **View Your Donations**
   - Click "My Contributions" in the navbar
   - See all your past donations
   - Track campaign progress

### Transaction Fees

- Small network fee (usually < 0.01 ALGO)
- Paid in ALGO from your wallet
- Goes to Algorand network validators

### Verifying Donations

All donations are on the blockchain:
1. After donating, you'll get a transaction ID
2. Visit [AlgoExplorer](https://testnet.algoexplorer.io/) (TestNet) or [AlgoExplorer](https://algoexplorer.io/) (MainNet)
3. Search for your transaction ID
4. See full transaction details

## For Campaign Creators 🚀

### Creating a Campaign

#### 1. Connect Your Wallet
- Follow the steps above to connect your wallet

#### 2. Navigate to Create Campaign
- Click "Create Campaign" in the navbar
- Or visit `/create` page

#### 3. Fill in Campaign Details

**Campaign Type**
- Select from: Housing, Meals, Medical, Education, Equipment, River Cleaning

**Campaign Name**
- Give your campaign a clear, descriptive name
- Example: "Build School in Rural Village"

**Goal Amount**
- Enter target amount in USD
- Be realistic and specific

**Profile Image**
- Add an image URL for your campaign
- Use high-quality, relevant images

**Description** (if available)
- Explain your cause
- Why you need donations
- How funds will be used

#### 4. Submit Campaign
- Review all details
- Click "Create Campaign"
- Approve transaction in wallet
- Wait for confirmation

#### 5. Share Your Campaign
- Copy campaign URL
- Share on social media
- Send to potential donors

### Managing Your Campaigns

#### View Dashboard
- Click "Dashboard" in navbar
- See all your campaigns
- Track donations and progress

#### Withdraw Funds
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Find the campaign
3. Click "Withdraw"
4. Confirm transaction
5. Funds sent to your wallet

**Important**: 
- You can only withdraw from campaigns you created
- Withdrawals are instant
- Transaction fees apply

### Best Practices

**Campaign Success Tips**:
- Use clear, compelling images
- Write detailed descriptions
- Set realistic goals
- Update donors on progress
- Share widely on social media
- Engage with your community

**Transparency**:
- All transactions are public on blockchain
- Be honest about fund usage
- Provide updates to donors
- Build trust with your audience

## Troubleshooting 🔧

### Wallet Won't Connect

**Problem**: "Connect Wallet" doesn't work

**Solutions**:
- Ensure wallet app is installed
- Check you're on the correct network (TestNet/MainNet)
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Transaction Failed

**Problem**: Transaction rejected or failed

**Solutions**:
- Check you have enough ALGO for fees
- Ensure you're on the correct network
- Verify wallet is unlocked
- Try again in a few seconds
- Check Algorand network status

### Can't See My Campaign

**Problem**: Created campaign doesn't appear

**Solutions**:
- Wait 10-20 seconds for blockchain confirmation
- Refresh the page
- Check transaction on AlgoExplorer
- Verify you're connected with the same wallet

### Donation Not Showing

**Problem**: Made donation but it's not reflected

**Solutions**:
- Wait for blockchain confirmation (5-10 seconds)
- Refresh the page
- Check transaction on AlgoExplorer
- Verify transaction was successful

## Safety & Security 🔒

### Protect Your Wallet

- **Never share your seed phrase** (12 or 25 words)
- **Never share your private key**
- Keep your seed phrase offline and secure
- Use strong passwords
- Enable biometric authentication if available

### Verify Before Donating

- Check campaign details carefully
- Verify creator's identity if possible
- Look for campaigns with social proof
- Start with small donations to test

### Scam Prevention

**Red Flags**:
- Unrealistic promises
- Pressure to donate quickly
- Requests for seed phrase or private key
- Suspicious URLs or links
- Too-good-to-be-true returns

**Stay Safe**:
- Only use official Donatechain URL
- Verify wallet connection requests
- Double-check transaction details
- Report suspicious campaigns

## Understanding Blockchain 🔗

### What is Algorand?

Algorand is a fast, secure, and eco-friendly blockchain:
- Transactions confirm in ~5 seconds
- Very low fees (< $0.01)
- Carbon-negative (environmentally friendly)
- Used by governments and enterprises

### Why Blockchain for Donations?

**Transparency**:
- Every donation is recorded publicly
- Track exactly where money goes
- No hidden fees or intermediaries

**Security**:
- Cryptographically secure
- Cannot be altered or faked
- Your wallet, your control

**Global**:
- Donate from anywhere
- No bank accounts needed
- No currency conversion hassles

**Instant**:
- Donations arrive immediately
- No waiting for bank transfers
- Creators can withdraw anytime

## FAQ ❓

**Q: Is this real money?**
A: On TestNet, no - it's for testing. On MainNet, yes - real ALGO cryptocurrency.

**Q: How much does it cost to donate?**
A: Just the donation amount plus a tiny network fee (~0.001 ALGO).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: No - blockchain transactions are permanent. Donate carefully!

**Q: How do I convert ALGO to regular money?**
A: Use cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance.

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**
A: Depends on your country's laws and the campaign's status. Consult a tax professional.

**Q: What if the campaign creator doesn't use funds properly?**
A: All transactions are public. Report misuse to the platform and community.

**Q: Can I donate anonymously?**
A: Your wallet address is public, but not linked to your real identity unless you share it.

**Q: What happens if I lose my wallet?**
A: If you lose your seed phrase, your funds are lost forever. Back it up securely!
